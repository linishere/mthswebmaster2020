About the Teacher: Ms. Wone


The teacher of the computer science pathway is Ms. Khin Wone. Ms. Wone started her career in computer science after moving to the United States from Burma. She started her new life on the East Coast before eventually making her way to the West. Already knowing the life of hard work and determination, Ms. Wone worked her way up to find herself a job in Microsoft. After that, she found a career in teaching, teaching high school mathematics at Lynnwood High School before taking the job at Mountlake Terrace High School and Mellowdale High School as the new computer science teacher. Currently, Ms. Wone seeks to enrich the learning environment of her computer science students, enrolling in several programs, and even receiving a certification in AP Computer Science Principles, allowing students to take an additional AP exam as they please.
