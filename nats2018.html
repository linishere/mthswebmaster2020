National Convention 2018 at Altanta

Over the summer of 2018, the MTHS TSA had another great showing at the national competition. Seven students and one advisor flew down to Atlanta to continue the energy of the past competition. After a stressful episode following a missed plane ride, the group made haste as they arrived in the middle of the event turn in. However, the team soon settled in and sought to do the best they could in their events. By the end of three days, two teams and one individual made it to the semi-final competition in their event: Sophie Burbank and Reiden Chea in Structural Design and Engineering, Matthew Khajehpour, Reiden Chea, and Tony Le in Animatronics, and Sophie Burbank in Dragster Design After another day of competition, the Animatronics team took 4th place in their event, an upgrade from the previous year, however, the Structural Design team was denied another championship title, instead settling for second place. Nevertheless, the team had fun exploring the city of Atlanta, venturing around the World Congress Center, and having fun at the Georgia Aquarium, World of Coke, and Six Flags. This summer, the team is sure to bring their all, once again, to the competition in DC.
