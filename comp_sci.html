Computer Science is for programming enthusiasts, and those who wish to get down with the wires! Computer science is the only STEM pathway to offer an AP course, being AP Computer Science, which students can take this as juniors or seniors. In this pathway, students will become acquainted with programs such as Processing, HTML, CSS XML/XSL, Java, Arduino, MultiSim, and more! On top of this, students can receive UW credit for participating in either Computer Science Principles, or AP Computer Science. Students work individually for most coding projects in this pathway, however, student collaboration is encouraged for certain projects. Students are mostly graded on projects rather than tests and quizzes, and all assignments are graded on a 20 point scale.
