We are not here to lead "students";
We are here to lead the next leaders of our time

Every year in June, right before the TSA national competition, the MTHS TSA elects a new board of six officers. Previous officers are allowed to re-run for election every year, and elections are decided by popular vote. Officers campaign to our school's TSA in hopes of gaining the most votes. Officers do not run for specific positions, but rather to be on the core council. Leadership positions are assigned after elections are decided. In addition, veteran members may be run for unofficial positions to allow new faces to take the role of leadership.
