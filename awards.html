Awards Throughout the Years

Throughout our seven years of existence, the MTHS TSA has racked up two trophy cases full of first, second, and third-place state finishes, as well as some national finishes. Our chapter works hard all year long to complete quality grade projects, meticulously inspecting our final designs before final submission. As a result, we have placed in many different events throughout the years as listed.
