National Convention 2016 at Nashville

In the year of 2016, the MTHS TSA only sent two students to the national competition, being sophomores Reiden Chea and Trevor Doan. However, despite the low numbers from MTHS, the duo established deep connections with the BTMS TSA who sent nine students. Nearly all of those students have since come to MTHS, and are heavily involved and committed in our TSA chapter. The dynamic duo brought home a fourth-place finish in Structural Design and Engineering, losing out on the third-place trophy by the narrowest of margins: 0.089 points. In terms of BTMS, Cole Johnston took home a second-place finish in Career Preparation, the Junior Solar Sprint Team claimed eighth, and the Leadership Strategies team finished in fifth. All of these students have since migrated to MTHS, where they continue to work hard to maintain their reputation.
