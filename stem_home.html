MTHS STEM Program

Our STEM program offers a total of three pathways for students to choose from. These are: Biotechnology, Aerospace Engineering, and Computer Science. Upon their first year in the STEM program, students will take the course Introduction to Engineering Design, or IED. After, that they can choose to participate in one or more of the three pathways for their next three years of high school.
